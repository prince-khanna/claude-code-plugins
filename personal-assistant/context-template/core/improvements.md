name: Improvements
description: Cross-project tracker for system-level friction patterns and skill improvement proposals. Enables active self-improvement by surfacing recurring issues and tracking proposals through to resolution.
update_policy: Add friction notes from retrospectives. Promote at 2+ occurrences. Update status on review/application. Archive when verified. No permission required.
---

## Friction Log

<guide>First-occurrence friction notes from retrospectives. When the same root cause appears 2+ times, promote to Active Proposals. Prune resolved entries periodically.</guide>

## Active Proposals

<guide>Promoted from friction log when a pattern appears 2+ times across sessions. Each proposal tracks a specific improvement with evidence, status, and affected area. Status values: Proposed | Accepted | In Progress | Applied | Rejected</guide>

<format>
### [ENHANCEMENT|NEW SKILL] skill-name — Short description
- **Evidence**: [Friction moments that motivated this, with dates]
- **Projects**: [Which projects encountered this]
- **Current behavior**: [What happens now]
- **Proposed change**: [What should change]
- **Affected section**: [Which part of the SKILL.md or system]
- **Status**: Proposed
- **Promoted**: [Date promoted from friction log]
</format>

## Applied & Verified

<guide>Archive of proposals that were applied and verified. An applied proposal is verified when no friction recurrence is observed in 2-3 relevant sessions after application. Prune entries older than 3 months if verified.</guide>

<format>
### [ENHANCEMENT|NEW SKILL] skill-name — Short description
- **Applied**: [Date]
- **Verified**: [Date] — [Brief verification note]
</format>
